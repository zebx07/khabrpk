// About Khabr

Most media celebrates technology. We ask what it actually means for you.

Pakistan has no shortage of tech news — funding rounds, product launches, official announcements. What it lacks is honest analysis of who all this actually helps, and who it leaves behind.

Khabr covers the human side of the AI age in Pakistan: jobs, education, freelancing, and the widening gap between hype and reality. We analyze, we question, we explain — and we hand the mic to the people living it.

The human side of Pakistan's tech shift. Real stories, real impact — no hype.
