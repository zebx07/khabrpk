Three free AI programs Pakistani students can actually join right now

No fees, real certificates, and open to people from any background. A practical guide cut down to only what's verified and currently running.

By Khabr·June 21, 2026·3 min read

Most "free AI course" lists you'll find online are half-expired by the time you read them. This one is cut down to programs that are verified and currently open — and genuinely free, not free-trial-then-paywall.

PIAIC (Presidential Initiative for Artificial Intelligence and Computing) runs one of the largest hands-on AI programs in the country. It's a structured, multi-quarter course that takes you from the basics through building and deploying AI systems — no prior coding experience required. With a six-figure alumni base, it's the most established option on this list. Enrolment is free through their official portal.

Virtual University offers a free "Introduction to Modern AI" course, developed with the Information Technology University. It's fully online and a solid, low-pressure entry point for anyone wanting to understand AI before committing to something heavier.

The ACT AI Program, a government-backed initiative, runs in time-limited batches across dozens of universities. It's open to students from any field, takes around eight weeks, and ends with a recognised certificate. Because it runs in cycles, the move is to check the official site for the next open intake rather than assuming it's always available.

None of these cost money. All of them give you something real to put on a CV. In a market where AI skills pay several times more than the work they're replacing, the barrier was never access. It's starting.
